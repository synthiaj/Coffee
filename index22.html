• Meaning: Doppio is an Italian multiplier, meaning "double".

  • Mix: Doppio is a double shot of espresso.

  • Facts: This beverage may be subject to variations in how much and how the traditional espresso. You can for example create a cold coffee variant by adding two or three cubes of ice into the glass. In this case, you need to sweeten the coffee first before adding the ice if you want it sweet.
